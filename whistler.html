Whistler

Learn more: here

Location: British Colombia, Canada

I visited Whistler in 2018 with my family and family friends. Throughout my time there I was in awe of the wrap-around views, not to mention the incredible skiing and bustling town. Whistler is the perfect combination of everything I could want in a ski resort. I would highly recommend everyone pay it a visit!
