Lifelong Skier

I have been skiing for as long as I can remember. My first time on skis was age 5. It quickly became one of my favorite pastimes and an incredible way to spend time with family and friends. While my time skiing today is merely recreational, I grew up competitively ski racing.

Role Model: Mikaela Shiffrin

Mikaela Shiffrin is an American Ski racer who won her first world cup at age 17. Since then, she has continued to dominate around the world. In addition to skiing, however, she is amazingly able to demonstrate humility, poise and authenticity. She has remained a consistent role model to me.


In my 15+ year long skiing career, I have gotten to explore a variety of mountains. I enjoy the sport, regardless of where I am, but that doesn't mean that every mountain is created equal. This website is meant to showcase a few ski areas around the world that are special to me.
